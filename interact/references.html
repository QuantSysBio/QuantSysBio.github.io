inSPIRE Specific Papers

Please cite the following article if you are using inSPIRE in your research:

Cormican, J. A., Horokhovskyi, Y., Soh, W. T., Mishto, M., and Liepe, J. (2022) inSPIRE: An open-source tool for increased mass spectrometry identification rates using Prosit spectral prediction. Mol Cell Proteomics. (doi: doi.org/10.1016/j.mcpro.2022.100432)

Another article will soon be describing the development of inSPIRE 2.0 and inSPIRE-Pathogen.

Tools used by inSPIRE
